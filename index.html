How to Play

  1. Your goal: finish with a higher score than the computer without exceeding 15.
  2. You start. Click +1 to +5 to add to your score. The opponent will also add to your score at the same time!
  3. When the total score becomes greater than 10, the turn switches to your opponent.
  4. Play continues until the opponent's total becomes greater than 10 (end phase).
  5. Scoring: Highest total <= 15 wins. A total over 15 is a bust. If both bust or tie, it's a draw.
  6. Use Reset Game to start over.
